My story: the long version

Though I self-identify as a map nerd, I’m a generalist who’s quite good at many things but not an expert in any one thing. Unless you can be an expert at asking questions, in which case I am definitely that. I ask the tough questions that everyone is thinking but no one wants to say outloud. I ask targeted questions to help people suss out what they really want or need. I ask questions about why we are doing something a particular way and how we are going to do it. I ask for help when I don’t know how to do something.

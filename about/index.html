TL;DR

I'm a map nerd, generalist, feminist, and teacher. I'm straightforward, detail-oriented, and thrive when I have a million things on my plate. I love people.

My guiding life principles:

  • Take calculated risks. What’s the worst that can happen?
  • If you don’t ask, you’ll never know.
  • Communication is key.

My story: the long version

Though I'm certainly a map nerd, I’m a generalist who’s quite good at many things but not an expert in any one thing. Unless you can be an expert at asking questions, in which case I am definitely that. I ask the tough questions that everyone is thinking but no one wants to say outloud. I ask targeted questions to help people suss out what they really want or need. I ask questions about why we are doing something a particular way and how we are going to do it. I ask for help when I don’t know how to do something.
